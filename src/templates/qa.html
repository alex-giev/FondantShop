Helpful Tips

Storage Tips

  • Keep toppers in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight
  • Store in an airtight container to prevent humidity damage
  • Avoid refrigeration as condensation can affect the fondant
  • Handle with clean, dry hands to prevent fingerprints

Placement Tips

  • Place toppers on your cake just before serving for best appearance
  • Use a dab of frosting as "glue" to secure toppers
  • Arrange toppers on a smooth, frosted surface
  • Keep toppers away from candles and open flames
